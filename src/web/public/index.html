Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about the service

Is the bot completely free to use?
Yes! You get 5 free downloads every single day. If you need more, you can invite friends using your referral link to earn +3 extra daily download credits forever, or upgrade to Premium.
Are the downloaded videos watermark-free?
Absolutely. All downloads are fetched directly from the platform CDN sources, meaning you receive original files without overlay icons or watermarks (especially for TikTok).
Do you store my downloaded videos on your servers?
No. We care about privacy. The video files are temporarily processed via our API to create a direct link, and are immediately deleted. We do not maintain any storage or logs of actual video payloads.
How does the Referral Program work?
Inside the bot, run the /referral command or open the web app to copy your invite link. When a friend clicks it and starts the bot, your daily download limit is instantly increased by +3/day per active friend. There is no limit to how many friends you can invite!
How do I pay with Telegram Stars?
Send /premium in the bot chat or click the Pro tab in the Web Dashboard. A Telegram checkout invoice will appear in the chat. You can pay instantly using Telegram Stars directly through your app store securely.
What specific links are supported?
We support standard sharing links from: Instagram (Reels and posts), YouTube (Shorts and video uploads), and TikTok (standard video links). Private accounts are not supported.
