Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about the service

Is the bot completely free to use?
Yes! You get 5 free downloads every single day. If you need more, you can invite friends using your referral link to earn +3 extra daily download credits (up to a maximum cap of +30 extra downloads), or upgrade to Premium.
Are the downloaded videos watermark-free?
Yes. All public downloads are fetched directly from source links, meaning you receive processed files without overlay watermarks (where supported by the source network).
Do you store my downloaded videos on your servers?
No. We care about privacy. Video files are processed temporarily over SSL to construct a download link and are cleaned up shortly after. We do not host or maintain permanent storage of video payloads.
How does the Referral Program work?
Inside the bot, run the /referral command or open the web app to copy your invite link. When a friend clicks it and starts the bot, your daily download limit is instantly increased by +3/day per active friend (up to a maximum cap of +30 extra downloads).
How do I pay with Telegram Stars?
Send /premium in the bot chat or click the Pro tab in the Web Dashboard. A Telegram checkout invoice will appear in the chat. You can pay instantly using Telegram Stars directly through your app store securely.
What specific links are supported?
We support standard sharing links from Instagram — including Reels, posts, and carousels. Private accounts are not supported.
