Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 20, 2026

Welcome to SaveMyReels ("we", "our", or "us"). We operate the SaveMyReels Telegram bot and accompanying web dashboard. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our downloading service, we collect minimal user details:

  • Telegram Profile Info: Your numeric Telegram User ID, Username, and First Name (used to register your account, manage limits, and authorize admin commands).
  • Referral Connections: The user ID of the person who referred you (used to calculate daily download limit bonuses).
  • Download Log Data: The URLs of posts you submit and the download timestamp (used to calculate daily usage limits).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the operational needs of the service:

  • To process video download requests and deliver media files.
  • To enforce daily download limits (rate limiting).
  • To award bonus download limits for successful referrals.
  • To handle Premium Pro upgrades via Telegram Stars.

3. Data Retention and Temporary Files

We do not store your downloaded media files permanently. Downloaded video files are temporarily saved on our secure host system solely for processing and delivery to Telegram. All temporary files are immediately deleted from our server storage once the delivery is complete or within 15 minutes of download.

4. Payment Processing

All premium payments are processed securely through Telegram's official **Telegram Stars** mechanism. We do not collect, process, or store credit card details, bank information, or other payment credentials. Transactions are entirely managed by Telegram.

5. Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your user records from unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wishes to delete your account data, please contact us directly via the bot support commands.
